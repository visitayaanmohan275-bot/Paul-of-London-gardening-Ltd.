Our Story

Building Beautiful Outdoor Spaces Since 2008

Guys Hill Landscaping Inc. was founded with a simple mission: to transform ordinary outdoor spaces into extraordinary landscapes that inspire and endure. Based in Calgary, Alberta, we've grown from a small local operation into one of the city's most trusted landscaping companies.

Our approach combines creative landscape architecture with sustainable practices, ensuring every project we undertake not only looks stunning but also thrives for years to come. We take pride in our deep understanding of Calgary's unique climate and terrain.
